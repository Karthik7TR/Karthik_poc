Objection to Plan
Comes now , a secured (unsecured) creditor, and files this objection to the confirmation of the debtor's Plan of Arrangement.
This objection is based upon the following ground(s):

1.

The plan is not proposed in good faith or any means not forbidden by law because the debtor has not fully disclosed all of his assets.
2. *
The plan does not propose to distribute on account.
3. **
The plan has not been accepted by the undersigned party, a secured creditor; the plan does not propose that the debtor surrender to the undersigned the secured collateral and the value, as of the effective date of the plan, of property to be distributed under the plan because the secured claim of this party is less than the present value of said collateral.

4.

The debtor will not be able to make all payments under the plan and comply with the plan.
Therefore, , a secured (unsecured) creditor, objects to the confirmation of this plan and prays that the plan not be confirmed.
This day.
Form signature block.
*
Paragraph 2 is appropriate for unsecured claims.
**
Paragraph 3 is appropriate for secured claims.
