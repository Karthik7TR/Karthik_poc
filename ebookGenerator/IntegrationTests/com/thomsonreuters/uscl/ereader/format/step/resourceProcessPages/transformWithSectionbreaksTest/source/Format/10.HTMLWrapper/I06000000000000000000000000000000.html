§ 1:14. Optional provisions of contract
For tax purposes, real property is placed in one of four categories.
The first category includes property held by a taxpayer for sale to customers. An occasional or casual sale is not covered, but once a taxpayer enters the business of selling real estate, he or she may become a dealer.
