501. ——Recusal of transferee judge, retransfer, bifurcation, severance, remand and retransfer of actions and claims
Two of five private antitrust actions previously transferred to the Middle District of Louisiana would not be remanded to the Eastern District of Texas nor would litigation be retransferred to that district, despite contention that the Louisiana district was selected because of original transferee judge's familiarity with the facts involved and that recusal of such judge and the intended dismissal of the actions other than movant's actions dissolved the rationale for conducting coordinated or consolidated pretrial proceedings in that Louisiana forum, where there was no assurance that the other actions would be dismissed and the class on whose behalf certification was sought in movant's actions included members of the classes in the other actions.
