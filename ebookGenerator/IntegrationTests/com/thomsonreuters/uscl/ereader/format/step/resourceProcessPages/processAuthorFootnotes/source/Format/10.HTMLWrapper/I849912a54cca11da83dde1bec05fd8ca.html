Part II. Damages Relating to Particular Subject Areas
Chapter 14. Attachment, Writ of Possession, Garnishment, Execution and Exemption
D. Execution
§ 14:23. Surplus proceeds
References
If after the sale of property under execution there is an excess of cash over the amount necessary to satisfy the execution, the cash shall be paid to the defendant. 1 If the sale was by credit and an excess over the amount needed to satisfy the judgment exists, a bond payable to the defendant shall be made. 2
