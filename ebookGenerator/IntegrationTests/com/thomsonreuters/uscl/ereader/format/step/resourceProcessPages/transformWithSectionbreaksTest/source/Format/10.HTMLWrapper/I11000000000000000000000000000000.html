§ 1:19. Residential dwelling units—Escrow deposits
THE BUYER OF A ONE-FAMILY OR TWO-FAMILY RESIDENTIAL1 DWELLING UNIT HAS THE RIGHT TO HAVE ALL DEPOSIT FUNDS (UP TO 10% OF THE PURCHASE PRICE) DEPOS- ITED IN AN ESCROW2 ACCOUNT. THIS RIGHT MAY BE WAIVED, IN WRITING, BY THE BUYER.2
