ยง 3:2. Spousal privilege
Confidential communications between a husband and wife are privileged communications in Georgia. 11 Historically, some courts have confused this privilege with a spouse's competency to testify.
2 The confusion probably stems from an era when the spouse of a party was not competent to testify as a witness. However, it is now clear that confidential communications between a husband and wife are privileged and being the spouse of a party is not grounds for the disqualification as a witness.
