§ 1:6. Applicability of Federal Truth in Lending Act, Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act, and Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act
The taxpayer is conducting a business and the ordinary and reasonable expenses of the business are deductible against income realized from the operation.
The second category1 of real estate is property used as a personal residence. This is technically a capital asset, which is the next category to be discussed.
Being a personal9 residence, however, causes different tax doctrines to apply. Gain on the sale of a residence held for longer than one year is taxed as long-term capital gain.
